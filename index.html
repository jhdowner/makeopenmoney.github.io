Technology

We build technological solutions that empower people in areas suffering from economic repression. We aim to create technologies and products that will be accessible and usable, wherever you are and whatever your situation.

Education

We create educational resources on how to access and use the tools that enable economic empowerment. Our goal is to present balanced information describing both the benefits and the risks of a variety of alternatives to mainstream financial systems.

Research

We study the awareness of and attitudes toward cryptocurrencies and related technologies. We partner with local organizations and grassroots efforts to conduct this research.
